Сheesecakes:

  • Time: 40 min
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Cottage cheese 500g
Flour 0.5 cups
Eggs 1 pc.
Sugar 100g
Salt 0.25 teaspoons

Step 1:Mash the cottage cheese well in a bowl.Add flour, egg, sugar, salt. Mix thoroughly.

Step 2:Sprinkle the table with flour and divide into pieces

Step 3:Fry the cheesecakes on both sides in a frying pan with melted butter.

Baursak:

  • Time: 80 min
  • Serves: 12
Ingredients Quantity
Milk 120g
Flour 1 kg
Eggs 10 pc.
Sugar 40g
Salt 15 g
Butter 40 g
Yeast 5 g
Vegetable oil 300 g

Step 1:Melt the butter in a water bath and cool slightly.

Step 2:Add all remaining ingredients except vegetable oil. Then add the sifted flour and knead the dough.

Step 3:Divide into pieces of 100 grams. Pour the vegetable oil into the cauldron and heat it up. Dip the pieces in the boiling oil.

Breakfast of cottage cheese and yogurt:

  • Time: 10 min
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
cottage cheese 200g
yogurt 100 ml
sugar 3 tsp.

Step 1:Add sugar to the cottage cheese. I like the sweet mixture, so I add 3 teaspoons of sugar. The number can be reduced.

Step 2:Pour in the liquid yogurt. I bought it with strawberry flavor, but any taste will do. Breakfast of cottage cheese will be varied if you use yogurt with different flavors and new fruits or berries every day.

Step 3:The curd mixture is well mixed until the sugar dissolves and it can be served immediately.
